About US

DSP Group, founded in 1987 by Davidi Gilo, is a leading provider of semiconductor solutions for a broad range of wireless communications and consumer electronics applications. The company has pioneered innovations in the digital cordless and VoIP markets and continues to develop cutting-edge chipsets and software solutions that shape the future of converged communications.Over the years, DSP Group has established itself as a trusted name in the tech industry. From revolutionizing the telecommunication sector with its groundbreaking products to forging strong partnerships with global manufacturers, the company has played a pivotal role in shaping the future of smart connectivity. At the core of DSP Group's innovations is its dedication to low-power consumption technologies, enabling the creation of energy-efficient IoT devices. The company’s Ultra-Low Energy (ULE) technology has set benchmarks in the industry, powering a range of applications such as smart home systems, wearable devices, and industrial IoT solutions. DSP Group’s strong focus on research and development has led to significant advancements in voice and audio processing. Their chipsets enable crystal-clear voice communication, noise suppression, and superior audio quality, making them a preferred choice for leading manufacturers in the consumer electronics space.

OUR HISTORY

DSP Group revolutionized the telecommunication industry with the world's first telephony answering device (TAD) speech processor in 1989. The company expanded its portfolio with the acquisition of Advanced Micro Devices' (AMD) 900 MHz technology in 1999. With a legacy of innovation, DSP Group continues to set benchmarks in wireless and semiconductor technology. In the early 2000s, DSP Group pioneered advancements in Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications (DECT) technology, which became a cornerstone for wireless voice and data solutions. This innovation laid the groundwork for the development of integrated chipsets that support high-quality audio, video, and data transfer, enabling smarter and more connected devices. As the Internet of Things (IoT) revolution gained momentum, DSP Group emerged as a leader in providing ultra-low-power solutions for IoT devices. Their innovative ULE (Ultra-Low Energy) technology helped drive the adoption of smart home systems and industrial IoT applications, earning the company accolades for sustainability and energy efficiency. The acquisition of DSP Group by Synaptics in 2021 further solidified the company's global footprint, combining advanced AI and voice processing technologies to cater to a growing demand for smart connected devices across industries. This strategic partnership has enabled the development of next-generation solutions for edge computing, AI-driven applications, and wireless connectivity.

DSP Group

Synaptics Incorporated (Nasdaq: SYNA) has finalized its acquisition of DSP Group, a move initially announced on August 30, 2021. This merger brings together the strengths of both companies, leveraging advanced AI, voice processing, and wireless technologies to power a wide range of innovative smart connected devices.

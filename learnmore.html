CEO

Ofer Elyakim

Ofer Elyakim is the CEO of DSP Group, a leading global provider of wireless and voice processing solutions. Under his leadership, DSP Group has focused on driving innovation in semiconductor technologies, with particular emphasis on the Internet of Things (IoT), voice, and audio solutions. The company’s advanced technologies are crucial in powering devices such as smart home products, mobile phones, and telecommunications systems. Elyakim has been instrumental in steering the company’s growth, ensuring that DSP Group remains at the forefront of these rapidly evolving industries. With his extensive experience in both technology and business management, Elyakim has played a pivotal role in advancing DSP Group's strategic vision. His leadership has fostered growth and expanded the company’s global market presence, making DSP Group a prominent player in the semiconductor industry. His commitment to delivering cutting-edge products has allowed the company to address the evolving needs of the connected world, positioning DSP Group as a key contributor to the development of next-generation wireless and voice processing technologies.

Our Key Technologies

DSP Group is a leader in developing advanced semiconductor technologies, with a strong focus on wireless communication and voice processing solutions. One of the company’s key areas of innovation is the Internet of Things (IoT), where DSP Group provides semiconductor solutions that enable a wide range of connected devices, such as smart home products, wearables, and automotive systems. These technologies help power efficient, reliable communication between devices, making DSP Group a crucial player in the growing IoT market. The company also excels in delivering low-power, high-performance solutions for Bluetooth, DECT, and Wi-Fi technologies, ensuring seamless wireless communication in both consumer and industrial applications. In addition to its IoT and wireless solutions, DSP Group specializes in voice and audio processing technologies, which are integral to the development of voice-driven applications such as voice assistants, smart speakers, and smartphones. The company’s voice processing solutions offer advanced noise reduction, speech recognition, and high-quality audio performance, ensuring clear and accurate communication in a variety of environments. DSP Group’s continued investment in these key technologies positions it as a key enabler of the connected world, providing scalable solutions that meet the demands of next-generation wireless and voice-powered devices.
